Shillong Airport Overview

Shillong Airport (also known as Umroi Airport) serves as the main air gateway to the charming capital city of Meghalaya and its surrounding scenic destinations like Cherrapunji, Mawlynnong and Dawki. Located about 30 km from Shillong city center, this airport connects travelers primarily to Guwahati and other Indian cities.
