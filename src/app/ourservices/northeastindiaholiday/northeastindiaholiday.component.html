Adventure in North East India

ADVENTURE
Adventure Activities in Sikkim:
Engage jumping, trekking, and mountain biking are popular activities in Sikkim, especially to places like Yumthang Valley, Zero Point, and Nathu La Pass.
Meghalaya Adventures:
Explore nature walks, caving, and rafting at places like Mawsynram, Cherrapunji, and Dawki. The living root bridges are a must-see attraction.

Best Time to Visit and Travel Tips

Sikkim and Darjeeling:
The best time to visit is from March to June and September to December for pleasant weather and clear mountain views.
Meghalaya and Assam:
Meghalaya and Assam are ideal to visit year-round, with monsoon months offering lush green landscapes and cooler weather in the winter months.
