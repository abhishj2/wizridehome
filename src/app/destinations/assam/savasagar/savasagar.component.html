Heritage & Cultural Experiences

SRang Ghar – The Royal Pavilion

The “Colosseum of the East,” an ancient amphitheater where the Ahom kings watched games and cultural events.

Talatal Ghar – The Underground Palace

A seven-storied royal residence with mysterious secret tunnels and Ahom-era architecture.

Shivadol Temple

The tallest Shiva temple in India, standing majestically by the Sivasagar Tank.

Joysagar Tank & Temples

The largest man-made tank in India, covering over 300 acres, with historic temples along its banks.

Best Time to Visit Sivasagar

  • Winter (November – February): Pleasant weather for temple visits and heritage tours.
  • Spring (March to April): Ideal for festivals and cultural exploration.
  • Monsoon (May to September): Lush greenery but heavy rains; heritage tours may be limited.
  • Autumn (October): Cool air with festive celebrations.

Travel Tips for Sivasagar

 
  • Carry light cotton clothes in summer; warm clothing in winter.
