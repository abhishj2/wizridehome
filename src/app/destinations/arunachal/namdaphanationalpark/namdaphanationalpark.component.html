How far is Namdapha National Park from Dibrugarh?

Namdapha National Park is approximately 160 km from Dibrugarh, around 5 to 6 hours by car.

Are Wizzride cabs available to Namdapha?

Best time to visit Namdapha National Park?

Winter and Spring (November to April) are ideal for wildlife spotting, trekking, and exploring the park comfortably.

Do I need permits to visit Namdapha?

Yes, Inner Line Permit (ILP) for Arunachal Pradesh and park entry permits are mandatory for visitors.

Are accommodations available near Namdapha?

Basic guesthouses, eco-camps, and forest rest houses are available near Miao and Deban (entry points of Namdapha National Park).
