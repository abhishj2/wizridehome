Why Wards Lake Should Be Your Next Travel Destination

Located close to Police Bazaar, Wards Lake is an artificial lake built during the British era, named after Sir William Ward. The lake is surrounded by lush gardens, cobblestone pathways, and vibrant flowers, making it one of Shillong’s most-loved tourist spots.

Whether you want to stroll around the flower beds, take a boat ride, or capture the city’s beauty, Wards Lake Shillong offers the perfect blend of peace and nature.

Why Visit Wards Lake?

Scenic Beauty

Enjoy the sight of blooming flowers, shady trees, and a sparkling lake that reflect Shillong’s natural elegance.

Historical Significance

Built over a century ago, the lake represents the colonial heritage of Shillong and its British-era charm.

Cultural Vibe

During weekends and local festivals, you can catch live music, Khasi cultural performances, and more.

Perfect Picnic Spot

Relax by the lake with friends or family, try boating, and taste local snacks at the lakeside café.

Top Attractions at Wards Lake

The Wooden Bridge

Walk across the signature arched wooden bridge that runs through the center of the lake—one of Shillong’s most photographed locations.

Boating at Wards Lake

Enjoy paddle boating on the calm waters surrounded by gardens and hills. It’s a favorite activity for couples and families alike.

Botanical Gardens

Explore exotic orchids, colorful blooms, and ornamental plants that make this area a photographer’s delight.

Café and Food Stalls

Relax at the small café serving local Khasi delicacies, snacks, and hot tea—perfect for chilly Shillong evenings.

Lumdiengjri Peak

Lumdiengjri Peak offers hiking trails with stunning views of lakes, forests, and valleys for nature lovers.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the best time to visit Wards Lake?

The best time to visit is between October and May, when the weather is cool and pleasant.

How far is Wards Lake from Shillong city center?

It’s located right in central Shillong, near Police Bazaar.

Are boating facilities available at Wards Lake?

Yes, paddle boating is available during the lake’s open hours.

What are the opening hours of Wards Lake?

The lake is open from 8:30 AM to 5:30 PM and closed on Tuesdays.
