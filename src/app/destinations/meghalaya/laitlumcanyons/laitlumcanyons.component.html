About Laitlum Canyons

Laitlum Canyons, meaning "The End of Hills," is a stunning natural wonder located near Shillong. With its majestic valleys, craggy cliffs, and expansive views, it’s a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers. The breathtaking beauty of the canyons makes it a must-visit destination for anyone exploring Meghalaya.

Whether you're looking for a peaceful escape or an adventurous trek, Laitlum Canyons promises to leave you with unforgettable memories. From the panoramic viewpoints to the charming Laitlum Village, there’s something for everyone at this scenic destination.

How to Reach Laitlum Canyons

By Air

The nearest airport is Shillong Airport (Umroi Airport), approximately 35 km away. From the airport, you can easily hire a Wizzride reserved cab to reach Laitlum Canyons in about 1 hour.

Local Cuisine of Meghalaya – Must-Try Delicacies

Laitlum Canyons offers a variety of authentic Khasi delicacies that you must try:

  • Jadoh: A Khasi-style pork and rice dish, rich in flavors.
  • Tungrymbai : A fermented soybean dish, unique to Meghalaya.
  • Dohneiiong : A pork salad with onions and spices.
  • Meghalaya-style Momos : Steamed dumplings served with spicy chutney.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the best time to visit Laitlum Canyons?

The best time to visit is from October to March, when the weather is cool and the skies are clear for panoramic views.

How difficult is the trek to the bottom of Laitlum Canyons?

The trek to the bottom is moderately difficult. It involves descending steep slopes, but the stunning views along the way make it worth the effort.

Laitlum Canyons is located approximately 20 km from Shillong, which takes about 45 minutes by car.
