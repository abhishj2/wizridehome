About Umiam Lake

Umiam Lake, also known as Barapani, is a stunning man-made reservoir located near Shillong. Surrounded by lush green hills and offering crystal-clear blue waters, it’s a perfect destination for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and those seeking tranquility.

Whether you're looking for a peaceful day out or an adventurous experience, Umiam Lake promises to leave you with unforgettable memories. From boating and water sports to trekking and camping, there’s something for everyone at this scenic destination.

Top Attractions at Umiam Lake

Boating at Umiam Lake

Umiam Lake’s calm waters are perfect for scenic boating, from shikara rides to speedboat adventures.

Water Sports

Umiam Lake offers thrilling water sports like kayaking, jet skiing, and speed boating amid lush hills.

The Umiam Lake Viewpoint

The Umiam Lake Viewpoint provides breathtaking panoramic views of the lake and surrounding hills.

Nongkrem Dance

Near Umiam Lake, Nongkrem Village showcases Khasi traditions and hosts the famous Nongkrem Dance festival, attracting visitors from near and far.

Lumdiengjri Peak

Lumdiengjri Peak offers hiking trails with stunning views of lakes, forests, and valleys for nature lovers.

How to Reach Umiam Lake

By Air

Local Cuisine of Meghalaya – Must-Try Delicacies

Umiam Lake offers a variety of authentic Khasi delicacies that you must try:

  • Jadoh: A Khasi-style pork and rice dish, rich in flavors.
  • Tungrymbai : A fermented soybean dish, unique to Meghalaya.
  • Dohneiiong : A pork salad with onions and spices.
  • Meghalaya-style Momos : Steamed dumplings served with spicy chutney.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the best time to visit Umiam Lake?

The best time to visit is during the winter months (October to March), when the weather is cool and the lake is at its clearest.

How far is Umiam Lake from Shillong?

Umiam Lake is located 15 km from Shillong, and the journey takes about 30 minutes via car.

Are there boating facilities available at Umiam Lake?

Yes, you can enjoy boating in both speedboats and rowboats at Umiam Lake.

Can I do water sports at Umiam Lake?

Yes, water sports such as kayaking, jet skiing, and speed boating are available for adventure enthusiasts.
