About Shillong

Nestled in the heart of Meghalaya, Shillong is a charming hill station that boasts lush green landscapes, cascading waterfalls, misty hills, and vibrant culture. Often referred to as the "Scotland of the East," Shillong’s colonial-era charm, coupled with its modern urban appeal, makes it a must-visit destination for travelers seeking both nature and culture. From breathtaking viewpoints and serene lakes to lively markets and soulful music festivals, Shillong offers a unique blend of tranquility and adventure. Whether you're a solo traveler, a couple, or a family, Shillong promises an unforgettable experience.

Top Attractions in Shillong

 

Umiam Lake

Umiam Lake, near Shillong, is a scenic artificial lake ideal for boating, kayaking, picnics, and photography.

Elephant Falls

Elephant Falls in Shillong is a beautiful three-tiered waterfall surrounded by greenery, great for photography and relaxation.

Shillong Peak

At 1,965 meters, Shillong Peak offers 360° views of the city and is perfect for sunrises and sunsets on clear days.

Ward’s Lake

Ward’s Lake in Shillong’s heart is a tranquil spot with boating, gardens, and a wooden bridge—ideal for families and couples.

Laitlum Canyon

Laitlum Canyon, Meghalaya’s Grand Canyon, offers stunning views, deep gorges, and green hills—perfect for trekking and photography.

Mawphlang Forest

Mawphlang Sacred Forest is an ancient Khasi site with rare flora, protected by local deities, where nothing can be taken by visitors.

How to Reach Shillong

By Road

Shillong is well-connected by road to Guwahati, Tezpur, and Silchar. The Shillong-Guwahati Highway (NH 6) offers a scenic and smooth drive, with beautiful viewpoints along the way. Wizzride shared cabs ensure a budget-friendly and comfortable journey.

By Air

The nearest airport is Shillong Airport (Umroi Airport), about 30 km from the city. However, most travelers prefer to fly to Guwahati Airport (Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport), which has better connectivity. From Guwahati, you can book a Wizzride shared cab to Shillong for a comfortable 3-hour journey.

By Train

The nearest railway station is Guwahati Railway Station, about 100 km from Shillong. From there, Wizzride offers shared and private cabs to Shillong.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the best time to visit Shillong?

The best time to visit is from March to June for pleasant weather and October to February for a winter experience.

How far is Shillong from Guwahati?

Shillong is approximately 100 km from Guwahati, with a 3-hour drive.

Are permits required for Shillong?

No permits are required for Indian tourists, but foreign nationals need an Inner Line Permit (ILP).

What are the must-visit waterfalls in Shillong?

Elephant Falls, Sweet Falls, and Crinoline Falls are top choices.
