Top Attractions in Mawsynram

Mawjymbuin Cave – Home to the Famous Shivling

Mawjymbuin Cave in Mawsynram features a natural Shivling formed by stalagmites and stalactites over thousands of years.

Mawsynram Viewpoint – A Scenic Delight

Mawsynram Viewpoint offers panoramic views of Khasi Hills and, on clear days, Bangladesh’s plains beyond.

Jakrem Hot Springs – Meghalaya’s Natural Spa

About 30 km from Mawsynram, Jakrem Hot Springs is a natural spa known for its healing properties and relaxation.

Krem Puri – The World’s Longest Sandstone Cave

Krem Puri, near Mawsynram, is the world’s longest sandstone cave at 24.5 km, with narrow passages and unique rock formations.

Lum Symper Peak – A Hiker’s Paradise

Lum Symper Peak offers offbeat trekking with 360° views of the Khasi Hills and is great for camping.

Khreng Khreng Falls – A Hidden Gem

Khreng Khreng Falls is a quiet, lesser-known spot perfect for photography and relaxation.

Umkhakoi Lake – Meghalaya’s Secret Reservoir

Surrounded by rock formations, Umkhakoi Lake is a man-made reservoir ideal for boating and family picnics.

How to Reach Mawsynram

By Air

By Road

Mawsynram’s Local Cuisine – A Taste of Meghalaya

Mawsynram offers a variety of authentic Khasi delicacies that you must try:

  • Jadoh: A Khasi-style pork and rice dish, rich in flavors.
  • Tungrymbai : A fermented soybean dish, unique to Meghalaya.
  • Dohneiiong : A pork salad with onions and spices.
  • Organic Meghalaya Tea : A perfect drink for the misty weather.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the best time to visit Mawsynram?

The best time to visit Mawsynram is October to April, when the weather is cool and dry. The monsoon (June–September) is great for witnessing heavy rainfall and lush greenery.

How far is Mawsynram from Shillong?

Mawsynram is about 61 km from Shillong, and the journey takes around 2.5 to 3 hours by road. Wizzride shared cabs provide an easy way to reach Mawsynram.

Do I need permits to visit Mawsynram?

No permits are required for Indian tourists. However, foreign nationals need an Inner Line Permit (ILP), which can be obtained online.

What are the best waterfalls to visit in Mawsynram?

Some of the most stunning waterfalls include Khreng Khreng Falls, Byrdaw Falls, and Phan Nonglait Falls.
