Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Mon from Dimapur?

Mon is approximately 280 km from Dimapur, taking around 9–10 hours by cab, depending on road conditions.

Is it safe to travel to Mon?

Yes, Mon is safe for tourists. Wizzride ensures experienced drivers and local support throughout your journey.

When is the best time to visit Mon?

March and April are ideal, especially to attend the Aoling Festival of the Konyaks. Winters are also scenic and calm.

Do I need any permits to visit Mon?

Yes, an Inner Line Permit (ILP) is required to enter Nagaland, including Mon. Apply online or through government offices.

Travel Tips for Mon Village

 
  • Carry a valid Inner Line Permit (ILP) for Nagaland; apply online in advance.
  • Carry cash; ATMs are limited and often non-functional in remote areas.
  • Respect local customs, and avoid photographing elders without permission.
  • Connectivity may be weak; inform someone before entering remote zones.
  • Wear comfortable shoes for village walks and treks.
