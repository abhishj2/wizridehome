Do I need permits to visit Mokokchung?

Yes, domestic tourists require an Inner Line Permit (ILP) to visit Nagaland, including Mokokchung. Foreign tourists need special permits.

Travel Tips for Mokokchung

 
  • Carry a valid Inner Line Permit (ILP) for Nagaland entry; apply online beforehand for convenience.
  • Pack light winter clothes, especially from November to February; evenings can be chilly.
  • Markets are mostly cash-friendly; ATMs are available but may be limited in smaller villages.
  • Book Wizzride cabs in advance during festivals or peak seasons to ensure availability.
  • Respect Ao Naga customs, traditions, and seek permission before photographing locals or cultural sites.
