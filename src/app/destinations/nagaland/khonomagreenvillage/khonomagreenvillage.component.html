Best Time to Visit Khonoma Green Village

  • Spring & Summer (March - May) – Cool and fresh, ideal for sightseeing and trekking.
  • Monsoon (July - September) – Lush greenery but slippery trails; carry rain gear.
  • Autumn (October - November) – Vibrant with festivals and perfect weather. showers expected.
  • Winter (December - February) – Crisp weather, great for quiet retreats and homestays.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How far is Khonoma from Kohima?

Around 20 km or 1 hour by road via Wizzride cabs.

Are Wizzride cabs available to Khonoma?

Is Khonoma safe for solo travelers?

Absolutely. Locals are welcoming and the village is very safe.

Best season to visit Khonoma?

October to April for best weather and cultural festivals.

Are there ATMs or network facilities?

Mobile network is limited. It's best to carry cash from Kohima.

Travel Tips for Khonoma Green Village

 
  • Respect local customs and avoid plastic usage.
  • Carry cash – ATMs are scarce.
  • Wear comfortable trekking shoes for trails.
  • Ask permission before photographing locals.
  • Pre-book your Wizzride cab during festivals.
