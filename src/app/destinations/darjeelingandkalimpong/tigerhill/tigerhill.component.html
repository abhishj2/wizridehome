Tiger Hill Darjeeling: Best Sunrise View Over Kanchenjunga

Witness the breathtaking sunrise over Mount Kanchenjunga at Tiger Hill, one of Darjeeling’s most iconic attractions. Standing at 2,590 meters above sea level, it offers a panoramic view of the Eastern Himalayas, including Mount Everest and Kanchenjunga.

About Tiger Hill

Tiger Hill, one of Darjeeling’s most famous attractions, is renowned for its breathtaking sunrise view over Mount Kanchenjunga. Standing at 2,590 meters (8,500 feet) above sea level, it offers a panoramic spectacle of the Eastern Himalayas, including Mount Everest and Mount Kanchenjunga. For those visiting Darjeeling, a pre-dawn trip to Tiger Hill is an absolute must.

Best Time to Visit Tiger Hill

  • Best Season: October to December (Clear skies for stunning sunrises) & March to May (Pleasant weather, lush greenery).
  • Best Time of Day: 4:00 AM – 6:00 AM (Exact sunrise timing varies by season).
  • Pro Tip: Reach at least 30 minutes before sunrise for the best viewing spot.

FAQs About Tiger Hill

What time should I leave for Tiger Hill from Darjeeling?

Depart by 3:30 AM – 4:00 AM to reach before sunrise.

Can I visit Tiger Hill during the day?

Yes, but the best views are during sunrise.

Is there an entry fee for Tiger Hill?

No, entry is free, but parking fees may apply.
