Batasia Loop Darjeeling: A Scenic Marvel of Nature and Railway

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Batasia Loop, where nature meets engineering brilliance. This UNESCO-listed railway loop offers stunning views of Kanchenjunga, Darjeeling town, and lush tea gardens.

About Batasia Loop

Batasia Loop is one of the most iconic landmarks in Darjeeling, offering a blend of natural beauty and historical significance. Located just a few kilometers from Darjeeling town, this railway loop is part of the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The loop provides one of the best panoramic views of Kanchenjunga, the third-highest mountain in the world, as well as Darjeeling town and its surrounding tea gardens.

What Makes Batasia Loop Special?

  • Panoramic Views of Kanchenjunga: The loop offers an unobstructed view of Kanchenjunga, especially at sunrise.
  • Engineering Marvel: A rare spiral railway loop, overcoming the steep gradient of the mountains.
  • War Memorial: The Batasia Loop War Memorial honors the soldiers who sacrificed their lives during the British-Indian War.
  • Tea Gardens View: The loop offers views of the lush tea gardens that surround Darjeeling, adding to the beauty of the area.
  • Toy Train Experience: A ride on the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway is a memorable experience for train enthusiasts.

FAQs About Batasia Loop

What are the visiting hours of Batasia Loop?

The Batasia Loop is open from 5:30 AM to 7:00 PM every day

Is there an entry fee for Batasia Loop?

The entry fee for visiting the loop is minimal, and there may be an additional fee for visiting the War Memorial.

How long does it take to visit Batasia Loop?

A visit typically takes around 30 minutes to 1 hour, depending on how long you wish to explore the area and take photographs.

Can I take photographs inside the Batasia Loop?

Yes, photography is allowed. It's an excellent location for capturing the beauty of Darjeeling and the surrounding landscapes.

How can I reach Batasia Loop from Darjeeling?

Batasia Loop is 5 km from Darjeeling and can be easily reached by Wizzride Reserved Cab, Toy Train, or local taxis.
