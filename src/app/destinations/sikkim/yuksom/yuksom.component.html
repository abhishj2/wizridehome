Best Time to Visit Yuksom

  • Spring (March to May): Pleasant temperatures and blooming rhododendrons make it ideal for treks and nature walks.
  • Summer (June to August): Slightly warmer with intermittent showers; greenery is at its peak.
  • Autumn (September to November): Clear skies, vibrant hills, and the best time for outdoor exploration and trekking.
  • Winter (December to February): Best Time to Visit Yuksom

Travel Tips for Yuksom

 
  • Carry a valid ID; foreign tourists need a Protected Area Permit (PAP)

  • ATMs are limited—carry sufficient cash
