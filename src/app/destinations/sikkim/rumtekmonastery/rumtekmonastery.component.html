Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How far is Rumtek Monastery from Gangtok?

Rumtek Monastery is about 23 km from Gangtok and takes approximately 45 minutes to 1 hour by car.

Are Wizzride cabs available to Rumtek Monastery?

Yes, Wizzride offers reserved cab services from Gangtok, Bagdogra, and Siliguri to Rumtek Monastery. Pre-booking is recommended.

What is Rumtek Monastery famous for?

Rumtek Monastery is the largest monastery in Sikkim and the seat of the Karmapa of the Kagyu sect. It's known for its Tibetan Buddhist architecture, spiritual importance, and scenic surroundings.

Best time to visit Rumtek Monastery?

The best time to visit Rumtek is from March to June and October to December when the weather is pleasant and skies are clear.

Are there entry fees or permits for Rumtek Monastery?

There is a small entry fee to enter Rumtek Monastery. No special permits are required for Indian or foreign tourists.

Travel Tips for Rumtek Monastery

 
  • Respect local customs; maintain silence inside the monastery.
  • Photography may be restricted in prayer areas—ask before clicking.
  • Carry light jackets even in summer; weather can change quickly.
  • Start early to avoid Gangtok traffic and enjoy a peaceful visit.
