About Pelling

Nestled in the heart of Sikkim at an altitude of 6,100 feet, Pelling is a picturesque town that offers unparalleled views of the majestic Kanchenjunga range. Known for its serene ambiance, lush green valleys, and cultural richness, Pelling is a favorite destination for travelers seeking peace, adventure, and a connection with nature. Whether you’re a history buff, a nature enthusiast, or an adventure seeker, Pelling promises a perfect mix of relaxation and exploration.

Top Attractions in Pelling

Kanchenjunga Falls

Kanchenjunga Falls in Pelling is a stunning waterfall from the Kanchenjunga glaciers, surrounded by greenery—perfect for photos and relaxation.

Pemayangtse Monastery

Founded in the 17th century, Pemayangtse Monastery is a key Tibetan Buddhist site with exquisite art and the seven-tiered Sangtok-Palri model.

Rabdentse Ruins

Once Sikkim’s royal capital, Rabdentse Ruins offer scenic trekking through forests and glimpses of the region’s historic past.

Singshore Bridge

Singshore Bridge, the highest in Sikkim and second in Asia, offers thrilling views of valleys and waterfalls from its suspension walkway.

Rimbi Waterfall

Rimbi Waterfall near Pelling is a peaceful picnic spot, with the nearby Orange Garden offering fresh oranges and scenic strolls.

Sky Walk

Pelling’s Sky Walk, India’s first glass skywalk, offers thrilling views of the Kanchenjunga range from a transparent walkway.

Khecheopalri Lake

Khecheopalri Lake, sacred to Hindus and Buddhists, is a serene forest-surrounded lake believed to fulfill wishes and stay leaf-free by legend.

How to Reach Pelling

By Air

The nearest airport is Pakyong Airport (near Gangtok), approximately 135 km from Pelling. Alternatively, Bagdogra Airport (160 km) offers better connectivity with major cities. Book a Wizzride cab for a hassle-free journey to Pelling.

By Train

The nearest railway station is New Jalpaiguri (NJP), about 150 km away. From there, you can hire a taxi to Pelling. Wizzride provides convenient cab services from NJP to Pelling. .

By Road

Pelling is well-connected by road to Gangtok, Darjeeling, and Siliguri. Private taxis, shared cabs, and state-run buses are available for convenient travel.

Best Time to Visit Pelling

  • Spring (March to May): Pleasant weather with blooming rhododendrons.
  • Autumn (October to December): Clear skies and mesmerizing views.
  • Winter (January to February): Cold temperatures with occasional snowfall.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the altitude of Pelling?

Pelling is located at an altitude of 6,100 feet above sea level.

Do I need a permit to visit Pelling?

No permit is required for Pelling, but permits are needed for certain restricted areas in Sikkim.

Are there ATMs in Pelling?

Yes, Pelling has a few ATMs, but carrying cash is advisable.

What souvenirs can I buy in Pelling?

Buddhist prayer flags, handicrafts, woolen garments, and organic tea.

Is Pelling good for family vacations?

Yes, Pelling is a family-friendly destination with attractions for all age groups.
