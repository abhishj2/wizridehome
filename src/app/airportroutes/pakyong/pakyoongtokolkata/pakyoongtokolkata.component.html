Q3. How much is the average fare from Pakyong to Kolkata flights?

Fares usually start from ₹2,500 – ₹5,500, depending on season and advance booking.

Q4. Can I book both a cab and flight from Pakyong to Kolkata with Wizzride?

Q5. Is Pakyong Airport operational year-round?

Yes, but due to weather and visibility in the Himalayan region, flights may be seasonal or affected during monsoon months.
