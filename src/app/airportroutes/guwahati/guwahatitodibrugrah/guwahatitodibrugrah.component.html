About Dibrugarh

 

Dibrugarh is Assam's industrial and cultural hub, often called the Tea Capital of India. The city is surrounded by lush green tea estates, the mighty Brahmaputra River, and rich cultural traditions. It serves as a gateway to the easternmost districts of Assam and Arunachal Pradesh.
