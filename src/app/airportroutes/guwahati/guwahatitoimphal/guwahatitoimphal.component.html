About Imphal (IMF)

Imphal, the capital of Manipur, is a cultural hub surrounded by lush green valleys, ancient temples, and natural beauty. Famous for Loktak Lake, Kangla Fort, and Ima Keithel (Mother's Market), Imphal offers a unique travel experience blending tradition and modernity.
