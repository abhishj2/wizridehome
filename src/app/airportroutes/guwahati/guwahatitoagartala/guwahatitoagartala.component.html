About Agartala (IXA)

 

Agartala, the charming capital of Tripura, is known for its rich royal heritage, vibrant markets, and scenic surroundings. From the majestic Ujjayanta Palace to the sacred Tripura Sundari Temple, the city offers a unique blend of culture, history, and natural beauty. It also serves as a gateway to explore unexplored gems of Tripura.
