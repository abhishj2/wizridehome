Why Travel to Dibrugarh?

 

Dibrugarh, famously known as the "Tea City of India," offers picturesque tea estates, rich Assamese culture, and serves as a major hub to explore Arunachal Pradesh and upper Assam. It is an important commercial center and a serene escape surrounded by the Brahmaputra River and lush green landscapes.
