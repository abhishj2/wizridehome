Common FAQs for Guwahati Airport to Shillong Travelers

Is there a bus from Guwahati Airport to Shillong?

No, Guwahati Airport does not have direct bus services to Shillong. You can catch a bus from the Guwahati Bus Station.

How long does it take to reach Shillong from Guwahati Airport?

The journey typically takes around 3 to 4 hours, depending on traffic and road conditions.

Can I make stops along the way?

Yes, Wizzride offers the flexibility to stop for sightseeing, meals, or photo opportunities along the route.

What is the best time to visit Shillong?

The best time to visit Shillong is between March and June (summer) or September and December (autumn) for pleasant weather and clear views.

Are there tolls along the way?

Yes, there is currently one toll booth along the route from Guwahati to Shillong.
