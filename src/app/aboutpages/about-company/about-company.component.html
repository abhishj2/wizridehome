Our Story

How Wizzride Was Conceived

In the bustling metropolises of Bangalore and the vibrant tech hubs of the United States, the Wizzride founders forged their careers as software engineers in esteemed multinational corporations. For over a decade, they thrived in the fast-paced world of technology, contributing their expertise to cutting-edge projects and global innovations. Yet, amidst the hustle and bustle of corporate life, a longing for something more profound tugged at their hearts—a desire to build something of their own, to make a tangible impact on society, and to solve real-world problems.

In 2016, fueled by a shared vision and a relentless entrepreneurial spirit, the trio embarked on a transformative journey back to their roots in the serene landscapes of North East India. Leaving behind the comforts of their established careers, they set out to address a pressing need that had long plagued their homeland: accessibility.

Returning to the North East, they were confronted with the harsh realities of life in the region. Despite its breathtaking natural beauty and rich cultural heritage, many remote communities were isolated from vital services and opportunities due to inadequate transportation infrastructure. Towns nestled amidst the hills, such as Gangtok, Sikkim, Darjeeling, and Shillong, struggled with limited connectivity to major cities and airports, hindering economic development and social progress.

Determined to catalyze change, our founders pooled their talents and resources to establish Wizzride — a pioneering venture aimed at revolutionizing inter-city mobility in the North East. Drawing upon their expertise in software engineering and technology, they envisioned a comprehensive solution that leveraged cutting-edge innovation to bridge the gap between remote communities and urban centers.

At the heart of Wizzride's mission lay a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction. Recognizing the transformative potential of technology, the founders envisioned a fleet of luxury SUVs equipped with state-of-the-art amenities, designed to offer passengers unparalleled comfort, safety, and convenience. Every aspect of the passenger experience was meticulously curated, from the sleek design of the vehicles to the professionalism of the chauffeurs.

As Wizzride took flight, its impact reverberated across the region. From bustling city streets to remote mountain villages, the company's signature luxury SUVs became a symbol of reliability and trustworthiness, connecting communities and fostering economic growth. For passengers, Wizzride represented more than just a mode of transportation—it was a lifeline, providing access to essential services, opportunities, and experiences.

Seven years on, Wizzride's journey continues unabated, driven by the same spirit of innovation, resilience, and commitment to service excellence. As the company looks to the future, its founders remain steadfast in their mission to make a meaningful difference in the lives of millions. With each passing day, Wizzride reaffirms its status as a beacon of progress and possibility, transforming the landscape of inter-city mobility in the North East and beyond.
