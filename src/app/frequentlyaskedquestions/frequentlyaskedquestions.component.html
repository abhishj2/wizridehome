FAQs - Wizzride Shared Cabs.

What services do you offer?

We provide various car rental services tailored to meet your needs, including inter-city Shared cab service, airport transfers, local sightseeing, and more.

How many locations do you serve?

We offer Inter-City Shared cab services on four routes: a. Gangtok - Bagdogra Airport/Siliguri - Gangtok b. Darjeeling - Bagdogra Airport/Siliguri - Darjeeling c. Kalimpong - Bagdogra Airport/Siliguri – Kalimpong d. Shillong – Guwahati City/Airport – Shillong.

Do you provide multiple pickup and drop points?

Yes, we offer multiple fixed pickup and drop points with fixed schedules. Please refer to our website or app for details.

What types of vehicles does Wizzride provide?

Wizzride offers SUVs (Innova, Innova Crysta) for routes across Shillong, Guwahati, Gangtok, Kalimpong, and Darjeeling. Additionally, we have a range of vehicles for reserved bookings, including hatchbacks, sedans, and other SUVs.

How many people can travel in a Wizzride shared cab?

Each Wizzride shared cab accommodates up to 5 passengers. Detailed seating configurations are available on our website and app.

Is it safe for female passengers to travel alone with Wizzride?

Yes, Wizzride prioritizes passenger safety, with dedicated teams ensuring secure communication throughout trips. Many of our customers are female travelers, who make up over 70% of our client base.

Does Wizzride provide assistance for passengers with special needs or senior citizens?

Our Wizzride team and drivers are committed to assisting passengers with special needs and senior citizens to ensure a smooth and comfortable journey.

Will the Wizzride cab depart on time if only one seat is booked?

Yes, Wizzride cabs prioritize punctuality and will depart on time, even if only one seat is booked.

What if I have a lot of luggage bags when traveling with Wizzride?

Wizzride allows each passenger up to two luggage bags: one up to 21.5 x 15.5 x 9.5 inches (55 x 40 x 24 cm) and a smaller handbag. Please inform us in advance if you have extra luggage bags.

What if I need help or have special requests during my Wizzride trip?

Wizzride drivers are trained to accommodate special requests. Please feel free to communicate any specific needs, and we'll do our best to assist.

Lost something in a Wizzride cab? How to report and retrieve it?

If you've misplaced an item, contact Wizzride customer support at +91 7478493874. We'll assist in locating and returning your item.

Do I need to book a separate seat for my child with Wizzride?

To ensure a comfortable experience for all, Wizzride requires a separate booking for children 2 years and older. Children under 2 may need to present proof of age if requested.

Can I bring my pet in a Wizzride cab?

Pets are not permitted to travel on shared cabs. However, for private cabs, we do allow pets but require the necessary vaccine certificate, if required.

Can I change my name or pickup/drop location on my Wizzride ticket?

Wizzride does not permit changes to name, pickup location, or destination after booking.

Can I change my name or pickup/drop location on the ticket?

Changes to a customer's name, the origin of travel, and/or the destination of travel are not permitted.

What if I miss my Wizzride cab?

If you miss your Wizzride cab and do not have your ticket scanned on time, it will be considered a no-show, and your ticket will be canceled.

Does Wizzride offer discounts for frequent riders?

Yes, Wizzride provides loyalty discounts for frequent riders. For more information, please call +91 7478493874.

How do I get a receipt or invoice for my Wizzride trip?

Wizzride automatically emails invoices. For additional assistance, email customersupport@wizzride.com.

How long does Wizzride's refund procedure take?

Wizzride refunds typically take 5-7 business days to process. For questions, contact customersupport@wizzride.com or call +91 7478493874.

Can I reschedule my Wizzride cab in case of a delayed train arrival?

If your train is delayed, Wizzride allows a 15-minute grace period after the scheduled departure. For longer delays, you may reschedule within the same day based on seat and cab availability.
