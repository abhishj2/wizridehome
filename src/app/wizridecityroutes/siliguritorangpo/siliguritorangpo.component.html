Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Rangpo in Sikkim famous for?

Rangpo is known for its scenic landscapes, the confluence of the Rangpo and Teesta rivers, and its proximity to the Sikkim border. It serves as a major entry point to Sikkim.

How far is Siliguri from Rangpo by train?

Rangpo does not have a railway station yet.

Which is the nearest airport to Rangpo Sikkim?

The nearest airport to Rangpo is Bagdogra Airport (IXB), located about 86.9 km away, with a 2.5-hour drive to Rangpo.

Is there a Rangpo railway station?

No, Rangpo does not have its own railway station.

How far is Rangpo from Siliguri by bus?

Rangpo is approximately 75.1 km from Siliguri by bus, taking about 2 to 2.5 hours for the journey.

Which is the ice point in Sikkim?

Tsomgo Lake (Changu Lake) is one of the famous ice points in Sikkim, known for its frozen waters during winter.

What is the name of god of Sikkim?

The main deity worshipped in Sikkim is Guru Rinpoche (Padmasambhava), who is regarded as the patron saint of the state.

Which part of Sikkim is beautiful?

Sikkim is known for its breathtaking beauty, especially places like Gangtok, Yuksom, Zuluk, and Pelling. The entire state offers stunning views of the Himalayas and lush landscapes.
