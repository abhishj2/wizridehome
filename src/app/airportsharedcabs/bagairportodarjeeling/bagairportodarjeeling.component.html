What is the best month to visit Darjeeling?

The best months to visit Darjeeling are from March to May and September to November when the weather is pleasant and the views are clear.

How much does the Darjeeling ropeway cost?

The Darjeeling ropeway ticket price is approximately INR 200 per person, offering breathtaking views of the valley.

Where can I buy the best tea in Darjeeling?

You can explore famous tea shops like Golden Tips, Nathmulls, or Lopchu Tea Estate for the best tea in Darjeeling.

How much is the Darjeeling joy ride fare?

The Darjeeling Joy Ride fares are around INR 100 for a short trip, offering a glimpse into the toy train experience.

What are the must-visit places in Darjeeling?

Key attractions include Batasia Loop, Tiger Hill, Happy Valley Tea Estate, and the Japanese Peace Pagoda.

Is Darjeeling a good destination for a family trip?

Yes, Darjeeling is perfect for families, with scenic spots like Happy Valley Tea Estate, Darjeeling Zoo, and Mahakal Mandir.

Can I visit Darjeeling from Gangtok?

Yes, the distance from Gangtok to Darjeeling is around 100 km, and there are several travel options available, including shared cabs and private taxis.

Which is the nearest airport for Darjeeling?

Bagdogra Airport is the nearest airport for Darjeeling town.

Is Bagdogra to Darjeeling bus available?

No, there are no direct buses from Bagdogra Airport to Darjeeling.

Is Bagdogra to Darjeeling train available?

No, there are no direct trains from Bagdogra Airport to Darjeeling.

How many hours does it take to reach Darjeeling from Siliguri?
